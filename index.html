Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ranchat! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect
  • a) Personal Information:
    • Name: Collected during signup and displayed to other users.
    • Language Preferenc: Used for matching users with similar preferences.
    • Sexual Orientation: Collected to personalize user experience and matching, if provided.
  • b) Messages & Media:
    • Text Chats & Video Calls: End-to-end encrypted and not stored on our servers.
    • Images: Sent as one-time messages and are not saved permanently.
  • c) Device Information:
    • Device Identifier: Collected to identify devices and send push notifications to users.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To facilitate text and video chat features.
  • To match users based on language, gender, and sexual orientation preferences.
  • To enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To send push notifications about important updates and activity related to the app.
3. Data Sharing & Security
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.
  • All communications (text, video, images) are secured using encryption.
  • Self-destructing images ensure that shared media cannot be saved or screenshotted.
  • Firebase Authentication is used to securely manage user data.
4. Your Choices & Rights
  • Delete Account: You can request account deletion via settings.
  • Control Your Data: Users can choose to stop sharing their preferences at any time.
  • Report & Block Users: If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, you can report and block users.
5. Third-Party Services
  • We use Firebase for authentication and data storage.
  • Video calls are facilitated using WebRTC, ensuring direct peer-to-peer communication.
  • Analytics may be collected via Google Firebase Analytics to improve app performance.
6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
  • We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via an in-app notification.
